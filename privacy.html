Privacy Policy

ZolomonPrints — Last updated April 2026

ZolomonPrints Pinterest Publisher is a private, single-account automation tool used internally by ZolomonPrints to publish product pins to Pinterest.

Data collected

This application does not collect, store, or share any data from third-party users. No user accounts, emails, or personal information are gathered.

Pinterest API usage

The app accesses only the ZolomonPrints Pinterest business account via Pinterest's official API, solely to create and schedule pins linking to ZolomonPrints product listings on Etsy.

Third-party sharing

No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.
